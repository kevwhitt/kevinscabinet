toms.com

URL: toms.com/#

Target Audience

From what I could see, their target audience was young, stylish Americans who like special styles and designs of shoes. The site showed their special products on their homepage, but other than that, everything could be found easily with a sidebar navigation. That made it easy to find what you were looking for without having being distracted or lost. The site was simple; that made it enjoyable. The colors were soft and the content wasn't flying out at your face. I feel it was good for mobile viewing. The site didn't not feel overwhelming with links and pictures, but rather it was simple. There were advertisements to their new releases and such, but they were clean and simple.
